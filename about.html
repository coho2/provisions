About Provisions

Provisions Bakery has been serving customers in Armory Square since 1988. We take pride in our products, produced from scratch, offering special recipes each day, and continues to serve great meals and baked good at reasonable prices Check out the changing artwork when you stop by for a visit. Provisions supports local artists by allowing them to display their artwork at the bakery and rotating artist every few moths. Our small, friendly atmosphere is great for an order to go or for a break from your busy work day. Provisions offers a great variety of baked goods, some changing daily. There are always a selection of oversized cookies, bar cookies, breads, muffins, scones, danish, and a sampling of delicious deserts.
